Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional quality and sustainable sourcing, Maison Fresh brings together the finest organic produce and artisan groceries in Toronto's historic St Lawrence Market.

Heritage Meets Innovation

Located in the heart of Toronto's beloved St Lawrence Market, we honor the rich tradition of this historic marketplace while bringing modern sophistication to organic retail.

Our carefully curated selection features partnerships with local Ontario farmers, artisan producers, and sustainable suppliers who share our commitment to exceptional quality and environmental responsibility.
